# Transport and directions

Getting around town and asking for directions are essential skills. China has one of the most developed transport networks in the world: ultra-modern metros, high-speed rail (高铁 gāotiě), electric buses and ride-hailing apps. In this lesson, we learn modes of transport, directions and expressions for finding our way.

**Transport verbs:**
- **坐 zuò** = to take (public transport, plane, boat): 坐公共汽车 (take the bus)
- **骑 qí** = to ride (bicycle, motorbike, horse): 骑自行车 (ride a bike)
- **开 kāi** = to drive (car, train): 开车 (to drive)
- **走路 zǒu lù** = to walk
:::

## Asking and giving directions

:::methode
**Phrases for asking your way:**

1. **Politely attracting attention**:
   请问... Qǐngwèn... = Excuse me, could you tell me...

2. **Where is...?**:
   ...在哪里？...zài nǎlǐ? = Where is...?
   ...在哪儿？...zài nǎr? (Beijing dialect, more informal)

3. **How do I get to...?**:
   怎么去...？Zěnme qù...? = How do I get to...?
   去...怎么走？Qù... zěnme zǒu? = Which way do I go to get to...?

4. **Indicating distance**:
   离这里远吗？Lí zhèlǐ yuǎn ma? = Is it far from here?
   走路要多久？Zǒu lù yào duō jiǔ? = How long on foot?
   大约十分钟。Dàyuē shí fēnzhōng. = About ten minutes.
:::

:::exemple
**Dialogue: Amara is looking for the train station**

Amara: 请问，火车站在哪里？
        Qǐngwèn, huǒchēzhàn zài nǎlǐ?
        Excuse me, where is the train station?

Passer-by: 直走，然后在第二个路口往右转，火车站就在左边。
            Zhí zǒu, ránhòu zài dì'èr gè lùkǒu wǎng yòu zhuǎn, huǒchēzhàn jiù zài zuǒbiān.
            Go straight ahead, then at the second crossroads turn right, the station is on the left.

Amara: 离这里远吗？
        Lí zhèlǐ yuǎn ma?
        Is it far?

Passer-by: 不远，走路大约五分钟。
            Bù yuǎn, zǒu lù dàyuē wǔ fēnzhōng.
            Not far, about five minutes on foot.

Amara: 谢谢！
        Xièxiè!
        Thank you!
:::

:::attention
**Comparing transport in Cameroon and China:**
- Cameroon: moto-taxi (bendskin), bush taxi, informal taxi → limited formal public transport
- China: metro in 50+ cities, high-speed rail (高铁) linking cities in hours, ride-hailing (滴滴), bike-sharing (共享单车)
- China invests heavily in transport in Africa, including Cameroon (see Lesson 3.5).
:::

:::retenir
- 坐 zuò = take (bus, metro, train, plane); 骑 qí = ride (bike, motorbike).
- Directions: 左边 left, 右边 right, 直走 straight, 前面 ahead, 后面 behind.
- Asking: ...在哪里？(where is...) / 怎么去...？(how to get to...).
- Key places: 火车站 (station), 地铁站 (metro), 医院 (hospital), 超市 (supermarket).
:::
