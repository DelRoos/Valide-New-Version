# Greetings and introductions

Knowing how to greet people and introduce yourself is the first step in communicating in Mandarin. In China, greetings vary according to the time of day and the level of formality of the relationship.

## Greetings

:::propriete
| Mandarin | Pinyin | Meaning | Context |
|---|---|---|---|
| 你好 | nǐ hǎo | hello | universal greeting, informal |
| 您好 | nín hǎo | hello (formal) | to an adult, superior — shows respect |
| 早上好 | zǎoshang hǎo | good morning | until around 10am |
| 下午好 | xiàwǔ hǎo | good afternoon | from 12pm to 6pm |
| 晚上好 | wǎnshang hǎo | good evening | after 6pm |
| 你好吗？| nǐ hǎo ma? | how are you? | asking after someone |
| 很好，谢谢 | hěn hǎo, xièxiè | very well, thank you | standard reply |
| 再见 | zàijiàn | goodbye | farewell formula |
| 明天见 | míngtiān jiàn | see you tomorrow | lit. 'see tomorrow' |
| 拜拜 | bāibāi | bye-bye | informal, between friends (loanword) |
:::

## Introducing yourself

:::methode
**Basic structure for introducing yourself:**

1. **Name**: 我叫... wǒ jiào... = my name is... / I'm called...
   - 我叫 Amara。Wǒ jiào Amara. = My name is Amara.

2. **Nationality**: 我是...人 wǒ shì... rén = I am... (nationality)
   - 我是喀麦隆人。Wǒ shì Kāmàilóng rén. = I am Cameroonian.
   - 我来自喀麦隆。Wǒ láizì Kāmàilóng. = I come from Cameroon.

3. **Age**: 我...岁 wǒ... suì = I am... years old
   - 我十五岁。Wǒ shíwǔ suì. = I am 15 years old. (岁 suì = classifier for age)

4. **School**: 我是...学生 wǒ shì... xuéshēng = I am a student at...
   - 我是初三学生。Wǒ shì chūsān xuéshēng. = I am in Year 9 (3ème).
:::

## Asking questions about someone

:::propriete
| Question | Pinyin | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 你叫什么名字？| nǐ jiào shénme míngzi? | what is your name? |
| 你是哪国人？| nǐ shì nǎ guó rén? | which country are you from? |
| 你从哪里来？| nǐ cóng nǎlǐ lái? | where do you come from? |
| 你多大了？| nǐ duō dà le? | how old are you? |
| 你在哪个学校？| nǐ zài nǎge xuéxiào? | which school do you attend? |
:::

## Polite expressions

:::propriete
| Mandarin | Pinyin | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 请 | qǐng | please / would you please |
| 谢谢 | xièxiè | thank you |
| 非常感谢 | fēicháng gǎnxiè | thank you very much (formal) |
| 不客气 | bù kèqì | you're welcome (lit. 'no ceremony') |
| 没关系 | méi guānxi | no problem / it doesn't matter |
| 对不起 | duìbuqǐ | sorry / I beg your pardon |
| 请问 | qǐngwèn | excuse me / may I ask... |
:::

:::exemple
**Dialogue: Amara meets Li Ming at school**

A: 你好！我叫 Amara。你叫什么名字？
   Nǐ hǎo! Wǒ jiào Amara. Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi?
   Hello! My name is Amara. What is your name?

L: 你好，Amara！我叫李明。很高兴认识你！
   Nǐ hǎo, Amara! Wǒ jiào Lǐ Míng. Hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ!
   Hello, Amara! My name is Li Ming. Nice to meet you!

A: 认识你也很高兴！你是哪国人？
   Rènshi nǐ yě hěn gāoxìng! Nǐ shì nǎ guó rén?
   Nice to meet you too! Which country are you from?

L: 我是中国人。你呢？
   Wǒ shì Zhōngguó rén. Nǐ ne?
   I am Chinese. And you?

A: 我是喀麦隆人。我来自雅温得。
   Wǒ shì Kāmàilóng rén. Wǒ láizì Yǎwēndé.
   I am Cameroonian. I come from Yaoundé.

L: 太好了！再见！
   Tài hǎo le! Zàijiàn!
   That's great! Goodbye!
:::

:::attention
- 很高兴认识你 (hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ) = nice to meet you — said at the end of an introduction, not at the beginning.
- 您 nín is the polite form of 你 nǐ — use it with adults, teachers and elderly people.
:::

:::retenir
- Greetings by time: 早上好 (morning), 下午好 (afternoon), 晚上好 (evening).
- 你好 = informal; 您好 = formal (adults, superiors).
- Introduce yourself: 我叫... (name) + 我是...人 (nationality) + 我...岁 (age).
- Politeness: 谢谢 (thank you), 不客气 (you're welcome), 对不起 (sorry), 没关系 (no problem).
- 很高兴认识你 = nice to meet you.
:::
