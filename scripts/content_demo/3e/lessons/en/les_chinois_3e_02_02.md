# Meals and food (饮食 yǐnshí)

Food is at the heart of Chinese culture — 'Have you eaten?' (你吃了吗？nǐ chī le ma?) is even a common way of saying hello between friends. In this lesson, we learn vocabulary for meals, food and expressions for ordering in a restaurant.

## Meals of the day

:::propriete
| Meal | Character | Pinyin | Usual times |
|---|---|---|---|
| Breakfast | 早饭 / 早餐 | zǎofàn / zǎocān | 6am – 9am |
| Lunch | 午饭 / 午餐 | wǔfàn / wǔcān | 12pm – 1pm |
| Dinner | 晚饭 / 晚餐 | wǎnfàn / wǎncān | 6pm – 8pm |
| Snack | 零食 | língshí | between meals |
| Afternoon tea | 下午茶 | xiàwǔ chá | 3pm – 5pm (tea culture) |
:::

## Expressing hunger and taste

:::propriete
| Expression | Pinyin | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 我饿了 | wǒ è le | I'm hungry |
| 我渴了 | wǒ kě le | I'm thirsty |
| 好吃 | hǎo chī | delicious (lit. 'good to eat') |
| 不好吃 | bù hǎo chī | not tasty |
| 甜 | tián | sweet |

| 酸 | suān | sour/acidic |
| 苦 | kǔ | bitter |
| 咸 | xián | salty |
| 淡 | dàn | bland/lightly salted |
| 香 | xiāng | aromatic/flavoursome |
:::

## At the restaurant

:::methode
**Essential vocabulary for ordering:**

1. **Calling the waiter**: 服务员！Fúwùyuán! (Waiter! / Excuse me!)

2. **Ordering**:
   - 我要... wǒ yào... = I want / I'll have...
   - 给我... gěi wǒ... = give me...
   - 一碗米饭 yī wǎn mǐfàn = a bowl of rice (碗 wǎn = bowl, classifier)
   - 两杯茶 liǎng bēi chá = two cups of tea

3. **Asking for the menu**: 菜单 càidān (the menu)
   - 请给我菜单。Qǐng gěi wǒ càidān. = Please bring me the menu.

4. **Complimenting**: 很好吃！Hěn hǎo chī! = It's delicious!

5. **The bill**:
   - 买单！Mǎidān! = The bill please! (common usage)
   - 结账。Jiézhàng. = To settle the bill (more formal)

6. **Paying in China**: people commonly pay with:
   - 微信支付 Wēixìn Zhīfù = WeChat Pay
   - 支付宝 Zhīfùbǎo = Alipay
   - Cash (现金 xiànjīn) is used less and less.
:::

:::exemple
**Dialogue at the restaurant:**

Amara: 服务员，你们有什么菜？
        Fúwùyuán, nǐmen yǒu shénme cài?
        Waiter, what dishes do you have?

Waiter: 我们有米饭、面条、饺子和很多菜。
         Wǒmen yǒu mǐfàn, miàntiáo, jiǎozi hé hěn duō cài.
         We have rice, noodles, dumplings and many other dishes.

Amara: 好，我要一碗米饭，一条鱼，还有两杯茶。
        Hǎo, wǒ yào yī wǎn mǐfàn, yī tiáo yú, háiyǒu liǎng bēi chá.
        Good, I'd like a bowl of rice, one fish, and two cups of tea.

(After the meal)
Amara: 很好吃！买单！
        Hěn hǎo chī! Mǎidān!
        It was delicious! The bill, please!
:::

:::retenir
- 3 meals: 早饭 (morning), 午饭 (noon), 晚饭 (evening).
- Key foods: 米饭 rice, 面条 noodles, 饺子 dumplings, 鸡肉 chicken, 豆腐 tofu.
- Hunger/thirst: 我饿了 / 我渴了.
- Tastes: 好吃 (delicious), 甜 (sweet), 辣 (spicy), 酸 (sour), 咸 (salty).
- Ordering: 我要... / 给我... + classifier + food.
- Bill: 买单！or 结账。
:::
