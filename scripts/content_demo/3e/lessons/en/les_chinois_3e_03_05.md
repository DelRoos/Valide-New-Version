# China and Africa — China-Cameroon cooperation

The relationship between China and Africa is one of the most important of the 21st century. For Cameroonian students, understanding this relationship means understanding the world they live in — and why learning Mandarin is a real and concrete opportunity for their professional future.

## China and Africa: a historic partnership

:::definition
The **Forum on China-Africa Cooperation (FOCAC)** was created in **2000** in Beijing. It is the official framework for China-Africa relations, with summits every 3 years alternating between China and Africa.

**Key figures in the China-Africa relationship:**

| Indicator | Figure |
|---|---|
| China's rank as Africa's trading partner | 1st since 2009 |
| Volume of trade (2023) | ~282 billion dollars |
| Chinese loans to Africa (2000-2022) | ~170 billion dollars |
| African countries with diplomatic relations with China | 53 out of 54 |
| Chinese companies active in Africa | More than 10,000 |
| Chinese nationals living in Africa | ~1 million |
:::

## China-Cameroon cooperation

:::propriete
Cameroon and China have maintained diplomatic relations since **1971**. China is today one of Cameroon's main development partners.

**Major projects financed/built with China in Cameroon:**

| Project | Location | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Memve'ele hydroelectric dam | Ntem, South | 211 MW — increases national electricity capacity |
| Kribi deep-water port | Kribi, South | New regional hub for Central Africa |
| Yaoundé-Douala motorway (sections) | National axis | Economic transport improvement |
| Bafoussam Omnisports Stadium | West | Renovated for AFCON 2021 with Chinese assistance |
| Yaoundé Central Market | Yaoundé | Supported rehabilitation |
| Sino-Cameroonian Friendship Hospital | Yaoundé | Medical cooperation |

**Trade**: Cameroon exports mainly **timber, cotton, cocoa and crude oil** to China. It imports **manufactured goods, machinery, electronics and textiles** from China.
:::

## Confucius Institutes (孔子学院 Kǒngzǐ Xuéyuàn)

:::methode
**孔子学院 Kǒngzǐ Xuéyuàn** (Confucius Institutes) are Chinese language and culture teaching centres funded by China and based in universities around the world.

**In Cameroon:**
- **Confucius Institute at the University of Yaoundé I** (established 2007) — first in Central Africa.
- **Confucius Institute at the University of Douala** — focused on economic exchanges.
- **Confucius Institute at the University of Dschang** — established 2012.

**What they offer:**
- Mandarin courses from beginner to advanced.
- Preparation for the **HSK exam** (汉语水平考试 Hànyǔ Shuǐpíng Kǎoshì = Chinese Proficiency Test).
- Scholarships to study in China.
- Cultural events: Chinese New Year, calligraphy, cuisine.

**The HSK exam:**

| Level | Vocabulary | Approximate equivalent |
|---|---|---|
| HSK 1 | 150 words | Complete beginner |
| HSK 2 | 300 words | Beginner |
| HSK 3 | 600 words | Intermediate (daily conversation) |
| HSK 4 | 1,200 words | Upper-intermediate |
| HSK 5 | 2,500 words | Advanced (newspaper, professional meetings) |
| HSK 6 | 5,000+ words | University level |
:::

## Why learn Mandarin in Cameroon?

:::propriete
**Concrete opportunities for Cameroonians:**

1. **Trade and business**: China is Cameroon's top trading partner. Speaking Mandarin = a major asset in import-export, logistics, construction.

2. **University scholarships**: The Chinese government offers **thousands of scholarships** each year to African students to study in China. Mandarin is an eligibility criterion.

3. **Local employment**: Chinese companies in Cameroon (construction, mining, trade) actively recruit bilingual Cameroonian Mandarin-French/English speakers as **interpreters, managers, coordinators**.

4. **Tourism and hospitality**: The flow of Chinese tourists and businesspeople in Africa grows each year — hotels, agencies and airports seek Mandarin speakers.

5. **Medicine and health**: Sino-African medical cooperation offers training in China for African doctors and nurses.

**Saying**: "Learning a language is opening a window to the world. Learning Chinese in Cameroon in 2025 is opening the door to the world's largest emerging economy."
:::

:::exemple
**Dialogue: a professional opportunity**

Amara: 老师，学中文以后可以做什么工作？

Teacher: 很多！可以做翻译、商务、教师，还可以去中国留学。
          Hěn duō! Kěyǐ zuò fānyì, shāngwù, jiàoshī, hái kěyǐ qù Zhōngguó liúxué.
          Many things! You can be a translator, work in business, be a teacher, or even study in China.

Amara: 中国政府有奖学金吗？
        Zhōngguó zhèngfǔ yǒu jiǎngxuéjīn ma?
        Does the Chinese government offer scholarships?

Teacher: 有！每年都有很多给喀麦隆学生的奖学金。努力学习，你就有机会！
          Yǒu! Měi nián dōu yǒu hěn duō gěi Kāmàilóng xuésheng de jiǎngxuéjīn. Nǔlì xuéxí, nǐ jiù yǒu jīhuì!
          Yes! Every year there are many scholarships for Cameroonian students. Study hard and you'll have a chance!
:::
